About Yambol

Yambol is a historic city located in southeastern Bulgaria. It is one of the oldest cities in Bulgaria with a rich cultural heritage and a vibrant history spanning thousands of years.

City Overview

Yambol is situated on the Tundzha River and serves as the administrative center of Yambol Province. The city has a population of around 70,000 people and is known for its well-preserved historical sites and monuments.

Visit Yambol

Explore the fascinating history and beautiful places that Yambol has to offer. Learn about the city's past, discover amazing landmarks, and experience the warmth of Bulgarian hospitality.
